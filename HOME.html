Wisdom College is a welcoming community for students from Prep to Year 12 located in kollupitiya,Colombo 03. We are a co-educational school providing personalised learning where each child is supported to thrive in all aspects of their lives... At Wisdom College we believe that each child has their own unique gifts and talents and we strive to provide them with an education centred around their individual needs. Our goal is to help every student reach their full potential by providing a holistic education with compassionate and skilled teachers, excellent resources and facilities, and an array of academic, cultural and pastoral opportunities..

We embrace learning and aspire to high academic standards. Our inclusive approach to learning means that all students are welcomed at our school and supported with a well-rounded education.
